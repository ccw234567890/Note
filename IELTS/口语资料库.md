## Part 1: 核心句型与表达


> [!NOTE] 一、10个万能句型
> 1.  **I find it + adj + to do sth**
>     > 例句：`I find it hard to focus when there’s too much noise around me.`
> 2.  **It is + adj + for sb to do sth**
>     > 例句：`It’s essential for children to learn how to manage their time.`
> 3.  **One of the best ways to... is to...**
>     > 例句：`One of the best ways to reduce stress is to take a walk in nature.`
> 4.  **The reason why... is that...**
>     > 例句：`The reason why I enjoy traveling alone is that I get to be totally independent.`
> 5.  **I tend to / I’m likely to...**
>     > 例句：`I tend to stay up late when I have too much on my mind.`
> 6.  **I’m the kind of person who...**
>     > 例句：`I’m the kind of person who needs everything to be organized, otherwise I get anxious.`
> 7.  **Compared with..., I think...**
>     > 例句：`Compared with watching movies at home, I think going to the cinema is more immersive.`
> 8.  **What matters most is...**
>     > 例句：`What matters most is whether you enjoy what you do.`
> 9.  **It depends on the situation, but generally...**
>     > 例句：`It depends on the situation, but generally I believe kids shouldn’t use phones too early.`
> 10. **From my perspective / In my opinion...**
>     > 例句：`From my perspective, art should be accessible to everyone, not just the wealthy.`


> [!INFO] 二、20个高级句型表达
> **用于表达观点和原因的高级句型**
> - **It is widely believed that...**
>   > 例句：`It is widely believed that technology has improved our quality of life.`
> - **There is no denying that...**
>   > 例句：`There is no denying that social media has changed the way we communicate.`
> - **What I find particularly interesting is that...**
>   > 例句：`What I find particularly interesting is that young people prefer short videos over books.`
> - **One key factor that influences... is...**
>   > 例句：`One key factor that influences students’ performance is the learning environment.`
> - **This can be attributed to...**
>   > 例句：`The increase in obesity can be attributed to unhealthy eating habits.`
> 
> **用于对比和让步的高级句型**
> - **Although it is true that..., it is also important to note that...**
>   > 例句：`Although it is true that online shopping is convenient, it is also important to note that it may harm local businesses.`
> - **Compared with..., ... seems more...**
>   > 例句：`Compared with traditional teaching, online learning seems more flexible.`
> - **Despite the fact that..., ...**
>   > 例句：`Despite the fact that city life is fast-paced, many people still prefer it.`
> - **While some people argue that..., others believe that...**
>   > 例句：`While some people argue that video games are harmful, others believe that they can improve problem-solving skills.`
> - **It is a double-edged sword.**
>   > 例句：`Technology is a double-edged sword — it makes life easier, but also more stressful.`
> 
> **用于强调和总结的高级句型**
> - **What matters most is...**
>   > 例句：`What matters most is maintaining a healthy work-life balance.`
> - **It is not just about..., but also about...**
>   > 例句：`It’s not just about making money, but also about finding personal satisfaction.`
> - **In the grand scheme of things, ...**
>   > 例句：`In the grand scheme of things, taking a gap year can offer valuable life experience.`
> - **This highlights the importance of...**
>   > 例句：`This highlights the importance of early education.`
> - **It goes without saying that...**
>   > 例句：`It goes without saying that good health is the foundation of everything.`
> 
> **用于引出例子和解释的高级句型**
> - **Take ... as an example.**
>   > 例句：`Take Japan as an example — their train system is incredibly punctual.`
> - **To put it simply, ...**
>   > 例句：`To put it simply, it’s about finding joy in small things.`
> - **In other words, ...**
>   > 例句：`In other words, people are working longer but earning less satisfaction.`
> - **This is best illustrated by...**
>   > 例句：`This is best illustrated by the rise of online education during the pandemic.`
> - **Such a trend can be observed in...**
>   > 例句：`Such a trend can be observed in many developed countries.`

> 2.  **Let me think...**
>     > 例句：`Let me think... I believe it was around two years ago.`
> 3.  **That’s a good question.**
>     > 例句：`That’s a good question. I haven’t really thought about it before.`
> 4.  **To be honest,**
>     > 例句：`To be honest, I’m not a huge fan of reality TV shows.`
> 5.  **Actually,**
>     > 例句：`Actually, I prefer working alone instead of in a team.`
> 6.  **You know,**
>     > 例句：`You know, sometimes I just enjoy doing nothing.`
> 7.  **I mean,**
>     > 例句：`I mean, it’s not that I don’t like sports, I’m just not very good at them.`
> 8.  **Like I said,**
>     > 例句：`Like I said, I usually spend my weekends reading.`
> 9.  **In my opinion,**
>     > 例句：`In my opinion, children should learn financial skills at school.`

>     > 例句：`If I’m not mistaken, the museum closes at 6 p.m.`

> [!NOTE] 国际关系政治话题词汇

> 3.  **Bargaining chip** - 谈判筹码
>     - **Manipulate ...to its advantage**: 操纵...以谋求自身优势
>     > 例句：The issue of aiding Ukraine is a bargaining chip the Republicans use to manipulate the budget plan to its advantage.

>     - **reinvigorate**: 重振，注入新活力
>     - **battered**: 遭受重创的
>     - **in the wake of**: 在...之后，紧接着
> 5.  **make another push to revive the floundering effort** - 再次努力以挽救停滞不前的努力
