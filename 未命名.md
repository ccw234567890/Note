# Listening into Writing 通用句型模板整理

---

## 1. 开头：引入话题与整体评价（Introduction）

**功能：** 用来总起话题，指出“有利有弊 / 有优势也有限制”。

- *When used appropriately, **[X]** can bring both significant benefits and serious challenges.*  
  > 适用：任何“有利有弊”的主题（LED、并联电路、电动汽车、静电等）

- ***[X]*** plays an important role in **[area/industry]** and has a number of important advantages as well as some limitations.

- ***[X]*** offers several key advantages over **[Y]**, but it also presents certain drawbacks that need to be considered.

---

## 2. 列举与结构引导（Signposting）

> 指示语的使用能显著提升连贯性与可读性，评分时很加分。

### 2.1 顺序连接词

- *First,… Second,… Finally,…*
- *To begin with,… In addition,… Ultimately,…*

### 2.2 多方面结构句

- *The discussion **centres on** three aspects: **[A]**, **[B]**, and **[C]**.*

- *In terms of **[A]**, …; with regard to **[B]**, …; as for **[C]**, …*  
  > 用在三段式结构特别稳。

---

## 3. 优点 / 有利特征模板（Advantages）

### 3.1 引出优点（总句）

- *One of the most important strengths of **[X]** is that **[clause]**.*

- *A major advantage of **[X]** is its ability to **[do sth]**.*

- *Another useful characteristic of **[X]** is that **[clause]**.*

- *A further benefit is that **[X]** allows **[group]** to **[do sth]**.*

### 3.2 解释“为什么是优点”（机制 + 结果）

- *This is because **[reason]**, which means that **[result]**.*

- *As a result, **[group]** can **[positive outcome]**.*

- *By **[doing sth]**, **[X]** helps to **[reduce/improve] [problem]**.*

- *This feature makes it possible to **[do sth]** without **[negative thing]**.*

- *This in turn leads to **[consequence]**, such as **[example]**.*

> 与样例句同型：  
> *By minimizing wasted energy, LEDs help lower… while also reducing carbon emissions…*  
> *This feature allows components on one branch to be activated or deactivated without affecting…*

---

## 4. 缺点 / 不利影响模板（Drawbacks）

### 4.1 引出缺点（总句）

- *Despite these benefits, **[X]** also has a number of disadvantages.*

- *However, **[X]** also presents several problems.*

- *Nevertheless, there are some important drawbacks that limit the usefulness of **[X]**.*

### 4.2 具体问题 + 影响

- *One major drawback is that **[clause]**.*

- *A second problem is that **[clause]**, which can lead to **[negative result]**.*

- *A further limitation is the fact that **[clause]**.*

- *This can cause **[consequence]**, for example when **[example]**.*

- *Due to this, **[group]** may have to **[extra cost / extra work / face risk]**.*

> 和 Electrostatic / e-vehicles 等样例的结构完全对应。

---

## 5. “双刃剑”表达（Two-sided / Double-edged）

> 适用：安静的车、明亮的灯、并联电路独立性、静电除尘、社交媒体、AI 等。

- *This feature is a **double-edged sword**. On the one hand, it **[positive effect]**. On the other hand, it can also **[negative effect]**.*

- *This characteristic brings both benefits and risks. While it helps to **[advantage]**, it may also **[disadvantage]**.*

- *The independence of **[feature]** is helpful because **[positive point]**, but it also makes it more difficult to **[task]**, which is a clear disadvantage.*

- *The same property that allows **[good outcome]** can also increase the risk of **[bad outcome]**.*

---

## 6. 因果 & 解释（Cause and Effect）

> 用来说明“为什么会有这个好处 / 危险”。

- *If **[condition]**, then **[result]**, which may **[good/bad effect]**.*

- *When **[situation]** occurs, **[X]** tends to **[do sth]**.*

- *Because **[reason]**, **[X]** can **[effect]**.*

- *For example, when **[concrete case]**, **[consequence]** may happen.*

- *This is particularly serious because **[explain the seriousness, e.g. people’s lives may be put at risk]**.*

> 对应样例：  
> *This is because the former could lead to incorrect calculations being made which could put people’s lives at risk.*

---

## 7. 成本 / 资源 / 经济影响（Cost & Resources）

> LED、并联电路、静电工厂等话题都可直接套用。

- *This reduces both **[cost type A]** and **[cost type B]**.*

- *Companies therefore have to spend large amounts of money to **[action]**.*

- *This can make **[process/product]** more expensive and less efficient.*

- *As a result, more resources are needed for **[manufacturing/maintenance]**.*

---

## 8. 设计选择 / 适用性（Suitability & Design Choice）

> 适合“在什么条件下这项技术是一个好选择”的总结，例如：  
> LED vs 传统灯、并联 vs 串联、某材料/方案在特定工况下是否合适。

- *In summary, **[X]** is a very effective choice when **[conditions]**, but it is less suitable when **[other conditions]**.*

- *Overall, if **[engineer / designer]** wants **[goal A]** and **[goal B]**, then **[X]** can be an appropriate choice of design.*

- *Therefore, **[X]** is particularly useful in situations where **[requirement]**, although its drawbacks need to be carefully managed.*

> 对应样例：  
> *In summary, if an engineer wants all components to have the same voltage, to receive different currents, or to be controlled independently, then parallel circuits can be a very effective choice of design.*

---

## 9. 总结收尾（Conclusion）

### 9.1 纯客观总结

- *In summary, **[X]** can **[main positive effects]**, but it can also **[main negative effects]**.*

- *To conclude, **[X]** has **[number]** major advantages in terms of **[A, B, C]**, but it still faces challenges related to **[D]**.*

- *Taken together, these points show that **[X]** has a strong impact on **[field]**, both in helpful and harmful ways.*

### 9.2 带评价 / 趋势判断

- *Overall, the benefits of **[X]** in **[area]** tend to outweigh its limitations, provided that **[condition]** is carefully controlled.*

- *Overall, while **[X]** creates several serious problems, it is likely to remain important because of its advantages in **[area]**.*

- *In general, **[X]** can improve **[aspect]**, but only if its risks are recognised and managed effectively.*
